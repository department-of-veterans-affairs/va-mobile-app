What's New​

Specific feature highlights as displayed in the app's "What's New" screen.

v2.65.0

Released on: 12/17/2025

What's New

You can now view your Travel Claims, which includes:

  • View claim status and value
  • View and download decision letters
  • Access additional information about next steps

v2.56.0

Released on: 8/5/2025

What's New

We added mileage-only travel claims submission to the Health section.

  • Submit simple mileage-only claims for past in-person VA appointments.
  • View submitted travel claim status and history from your past appointment.
  • No paperwork or waiting—just a few taps.

v2.23.0

Released on: 3/5/2024

What's New

We updated how we filter and sort your prescriptions. Your active prescriptions will now show at the top of your list. You can still filter and sort your list how you want.

v2.13.0

Released on: 10/3/2023

What's New

You can now show proof of your Veteran status with the app.

v2.3.0

Released on: 5/10/2023

What's New

You can now add and edit your preferred name and gender identity in your Profile section.

v2.2.0

Released on: 5/2/2023

What's New

You can now get claim decision letters in the app.

v2.0.0

Released on: 4/4/2023

What's New

We updated sections to Home, Benefits, Health, and Payments.

  • Profile is now on Home screen.
  • Disability rating and VA letters are now in Benefits.
  • Direct deposit is now in Payments.

Release Notes​

A historical log of the raw release notes submitted to the App Store and Google Play.

v2.65.0

Released on: 12/17/2025

Release Notes

We have expanded Travel Claims functionality which includes viewing their status and value, reading and downloading decision letters, and accessing additional information about next steps. We also fixed a few bugs.

v2.60.0

Released on: 9/30/2025

Release Notes

We updated the app with improvements to performance, guidance, and support: Claims History now loads closed claims separately, improving performance and ensuring data displays as expected. Added technical support content to the Contact VA page to make it easier to get help when needed.

v2.59.0

Released on: 9/16/2025

Release Notes

We updated the app with improvements to appointments and accessibility: - For Cerner appointments, non-existent clinic and location fields are now hidden. - Improved accessibility in secure messaging so screen readers announce the selected recipient and provide a clear way to confirm the choice.

v2.58.0

Released on: 9/2/2025

Release Notes

We updated the app with several improvements to secure messaging, privacy, and usability: - Improved functionality to clearly direct Oracle Health users to health tools - Simplified secure messaging with auto-filled care systems and no duplicate recipients - Removed in-app survey comments to better protect Veteran privacy - Improved timezone display for Asia and Pacific regions

v2.57.0

Released on: 8/12/2025

Release Notes

We added mileage-only travel claims submission to the Health section. - Submit simple mileage-only claims for past in-person VA appointments. - View submitted travel claim status and history from your past appointment. - No paperwork or waiting—just a few taps. We also fixed an bug in secure messaging so that users will only see providers who are in their care system.

v2.56.0

Released on: 8/5/2025

Release Notes

We added mileage-only travel claims submission to the Health section. - Submit simple mileage-only claims for past in-person VA appointments. - View submitted travel claim status and history from your past appointment. - No paperwork or waiting—just a few taps.

v2.55.0

Released on: 7/23/2025

Release Notes

We updated the app to fix screen reader focus issues on iOS and ensure deep links direct users to the correct page after login. We also improved the styling of the in-app feedback feature for better readability.

v2.54.0

Released on: 7/8/2025

Release Notes

This release introduces a new in-app feedback survey located in the profile for Veterans to share general app feedback. We also added a “Dismiss” button to the non-VA meds alert, limited its display to relevant users, and updated the appointment reschedule link to show only for eligible appointments.

v2.53.0

Released on: 6/24/2025

Release Notes

App updates include unique file names for decision letters and visibility of disability ratings even when set to 0%. Pagination was added to the claim letters page, and the Review Letters detail page is now aligned with the web experience. Bugs affecting the Rx Refill Request floating button and animations have been resolved.

v2.52.0

Released on: 6/10/2025

Release Notes

We added an explanation to the Claim Issues tab and updated the listed issues to match the web version. Read receipts now appear in the message list and detail views. Users will see an alert if they try to upload an encrypted or password-protected document. Bugs with the "Start refill request" button on the Prescriptions page have been fixed.

v2.51.0

Released on: 5/27/2025

Release Notes

App updates include a weblink added on the Prescriptions page to view non-VA medications and general bug fixes.

v2.50.0

Released on: 5/13/2025

Release Notes

We updated the payment listed on the homescreen to show the latest monthly payment and includes an option to view the breakdown of that payment. We fixed a few bugs and UI issues.

v2.49.0

Released on: 4/29/2025

Release Notes

Users can now seamlessly access specific VA.gov web views of Notification Preferences and Claims without needing to log in again. On the Home Screen, Disability Rating and Latest Payment information are now hidden by default, with the option to toggle visibility. Updates to the Veteran Status Card also make it easier to access and display your Veteran status.

v2.48.0

Released on: 4/15/2025

Release Notes

We have enhanced the Veteran Status Card with a new API and clearer error messages. Other changes include consistent alert styling, removal of an incorrect link on the RX screen, and accessibility fixes for screen reader support and UI colors.

Release Notes

v2.0.0

Released on: 4/4/2023

Release Notes

We’ve updated where you find tools and information in the app. When you sign in, you’ll go to a new Home screen. On most screens in the app, you’ll find buttons at the bottom to select these 4 sections: Home, Benefits, Health, and Payments. Here’s what’s in each section: - The Home section shows your name and branch of service. It also includes links for how to contact us, find VA locations, and get updated information on COVID-19. - The Benefits section includes your disability rating, claims and appeals, and VA letters. - The Health section includes your VA health care appointments, secure messages, and vaccine records. - The Payments section includes your VA payment history and direct deposit information. We moved the Profile section to the top right area of the Home screen. Your Profile section includes your personal, contact, and military information. You can also manage the app’s settings in the Profile section.
